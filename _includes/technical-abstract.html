Technical Overview

Successful construction management relies on interoperability, accurate cost estimation and scheduling. While OpenBIM aims to standardize workflows, current industry practices still suffer from fragmented processes and data loss. This research proposes a computational framework to optimize 4D and 5D BIM workflows using IFC as a single source of truth.
